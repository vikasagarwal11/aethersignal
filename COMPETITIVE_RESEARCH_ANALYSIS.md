## 13. VENDOR-AGNOSTIC DATA MODEL & FIELD MAPPING

One of AetherSignal‚Äôs most defensible advantages is being **vendor‚Äëagnostic** and **schema‚Äëflexible**.

### 13.1 Problem with Fixed Schemas

- Traditional systems (Argus/Vault/ArisGlobal) assume **one fixed data model**.
- In reality, safety data comes from:
  - FAERS/VAERS/CAERS and other public sources.
  - CRO‚Äëspecific formats.
  - Excel spreadsheets and legacy exports.
  - Social media / call center / CRM systems.

### 13.2 AetherSignal‚Äôs Approach

- Maintain an internal **canonical safety schema** (case ID, drug, reaction, seriousness, dates, demographics, outcome, source, etc.).
- Allow users to:
  - Upload **any tabular dataset** (CSV/Excel/FAERS‚Äëlike).
  - **Inspect detected columns** (auto‚Äëmapping).
  - **Manually map** their columns to the canonical schema:
    - `case_id`, `patient_id`, `drug_name`, `reaction_term`, `seriousness`, `country`, `age`, `sex`, `onset_date`, `report_date`, etc.
  - Save mappings as **reusable templates** per source/vendor (future enhancement).

### 13.3 Benefits

- Removes dependency on any **pre‚Äëbuilt structure or single vendor**.
- Makes AetherSignal a **true ingestion/analytics layer** on top of *any* safety dataset.
- Supports a roadmap where:
  - New schemas (e.g., E2B imports, local HA formats) require **configuration**, not major code changes.

## 14. VALIDATION & LIMITATIONS (NLP + SOCIAL AE)

To be credible with pharma/regulators, AetherSignal must be transparent about **accuracy and limitations**.

### 14.1 NLP Query & MedDRA Mapping

- Maintain a **benchmark set of 20‚Äì30 standard safety questions**, such as:
  - "Show me serious hepatic reactions for Drug X in patients >65 in the last 2 years."
  - "Which AEs have increased by >50% for Drug Y vs last year?"
- Measure:
  - **Parsing accuracy**: How often the filters match the intended query (MedDRA LLT/PT, seriousness, time windows).
  - **Precision/recall** on a held‚Äëout test dataset (e.g., FAERS slices).
- Document:
  - Known failure modes (ambiguous queries, missing context).
  - Guardrails (e.g., show the interpreted filters back to the user for confirmation).

### 14.2 Social AE Limitations (Critical for Trust)

Be explicit that social AE is **hypothesis‚Äëgenerating**, not confirmatory:

- High **false positive rate** (sarcasm, off‚Äëlabel chatter, rumors).
- **No causal confirmation** ‚Äî mentions do not equal causality.
- **Duplicate posts** across platforms and re‚Äëshares.
- Limited **clinical detail** compared to ICSRs.
- Potential **demographic and geographic bias**.

Include in product copy:

> "Social AE insights are **exploratory signals** only and must be validated against structured safety data and clinical context."
